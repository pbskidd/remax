Give More: Closing Gifts That Give Back

Partner with Ecuadane to support Children's Miracle Network Hospitals while strengthening your client relationships. Our unique program allows you to make a meaningful difference with every closing gift you give.

A Partnership That Matters

Ecuadane is excited to share something special with the RE/MAX community. At Ecuadane, we deeply admire RE/MAX's commitment to the Children's Miracle Network, and we want to help amplify that impact. Our Give More program creates a powerful synergy between your closing gifts and your philanthropic values, allowing you to extend your positive influence beyond the immediate transaction.

How It Works

Starting today, every time a RE/MAX agent purchases one of our luxury blankets—whether it's a stock throw, a RE/MAX exclusive design, or a fully custom gift—we'll donate a full-priced blanket to a child in the Children's Miracle Network. That's right: one blanket for you, one gifted to provide comfort and security to a deserving child.

Beyond One-for-One

Regardless of your RE/MAX discount on what you buy, this donation is based on the full retail value of the gift. It's even more than a one-for-one deal—it's our way of standing beside you in the incredible work you do, both for your clients and your community. This generous approach ensures maximum impact for children in need while maintaining the exceptional quality of your client gifts.

Making a Difference Together

Comfort in Challenging Times

The blankets donated through the Give More program provide more than just physical warmth—they offer emotional comfort to children facing medical challenges. Hospital environments can be intimidating for young patients, but having a soft, beautiful blanket of their own creates a sense of security and normalcy during difficult times. Your participation in this program helps transform the hospital experience for these children.

RE/MAX's Legacy of Giving

Since 1992, RE/MAX agents have donated more than $185 million to Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. The Give More program builds upon this incredible legacy, providing a new avenue for agents to contribute while also enhancing their business through meaningful client gifts. This dual-purpose approach aligns perfectly with RE/MAX's values of professional excellence and community service.

Join the Movement

By choosing Ecuadane blankets for your closing gifts, you're joining a growing community of RE/MAX professionals who are leveraging their business success to create positive change. Together, we can make a significant impact on the lives of children while building stronger, more meaningful relationships with your clients. It's a win-win approach that reflects the best of what the RE/MAX community stands for.
