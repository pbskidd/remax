'Give-More' Program

1-for-1 Donation Matching

For every blanket you purchase, we donate a full-priced blanket to a child in the Children's Miracle Network. That's right: one blanket for you, one gifted to provide comfort and security to a deserving child.

Closing Gifts ...That Give Back

Partner with Ecuadane to support Children's Miracle Network Hospitals with every blanket purchase. As a RE/MAX agent, you already understand the importance of giving back to the community. Our Give More program aligns perfectly with RE/MAX's longstanding commitment to Children's Miracle Network Hospitals.

Double Your Impact

Regardless of your RE/MAX discount, this donation is based on the full $125 retail value of the gift. It's more than a one-for-one deal—it's our way of standing beside you in the incredible work you do, both for your clients and your community.


Impress clients while bringing comfort to a child in need.

Partnerships Matter

Ecuadane is excited to share something special with the RE/MAX community. At Ecuadane, we deeply admire RE/MAX's commitment to the Children's Miracle Network Hospitals, and we want to help amplify that impact. Our Give More program creates a powerful synergy between your closing gifts and your philanthropic values, allowing you to extend your positive influence beyond the immediate transaction.

Let's Make A Difference

Comfort Children

The blankets donated through the Give More program provide more than just physical warmth—they offer emotional comfort to children facing medical challenges. Hospital environments can be intimidating for young patients, but having a soft, beautiful blanket of their own creates a sense of security and normalcy during difficult times. Your participation in this program helps transform the hospital experience for these children.

RE/MAX's Legacy of Giving

Since 1992, RE/MAX agents have donated more than $185 million to Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. The Give More program builds upon this incredible legacy, providing a new avenue for agents to contribute while also enhancing their business through meaningful client gifts. This dual-purpose approach aligns perfectly with RE/MAX's values of professional excellence and community service.

Join the Movement

By choosing Ecuadane blankets for your closing gifts, you're joining a growing community of RE/MAX professionals who are leveraging their business success to create positive change. Together, we can make a significant impact on the lives of children while building stronger, more meaningful relationships with your clients. It's a win-win approach that reflects the best of what the RE/MAX community stands for.
